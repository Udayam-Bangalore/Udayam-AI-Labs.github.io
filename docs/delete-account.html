Account Deletion

Account Deletion – Longevix

How to Delete Your Account

To delete your account and associated data, please follow one of the methods below:

Option 1: In-App Deletion

  1. Log in to the app
  2. Go to Settings → Delete Account

Option 2: Email Request

Email us at support@longevix.com from your registered email address.

What We Delete

When you request account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Profile info – All personal information associated with your account
  • Email/phone – Your contact information
  • Uploaded content – Any files, images, or media you have uploaded
  • Usage data – Your activity history and behavioral data

What We May Retain

For legal, security, and compliance purposes, certain data may be retained for a limited period:

  • Logs for 30 days – We may retain system logs for up to 30 days for legal and security purposes
